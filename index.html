Training for Research Students

Increasingly, a doctoral degree involves developing the skills necessary to become an independent researcher, and not just making an outstanding contribution to your scientific community. This page contains information about the training opportunities available to Research Students in the department, and is maintained by the Research Student Training Officer (currently Louis Rose). Please get in touch if you wish to discuss any aspect of your training as a Research Student.

Upcoming Skillsforge Events

Skillsforge is a suite of online tools that assists research students in their training and evaluation of their practice. For example, you can use Skillsforge to record a research journal and to locate and register for training courses run by the university.
